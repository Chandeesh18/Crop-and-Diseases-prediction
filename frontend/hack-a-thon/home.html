ABOUT US

The Farm Friend gives the suggestion of which crop can be grown to get maximum vegetation taking in account the nature of the soil and region. Based on the crop and the soil fertilizers are also suggested to improve the mineral values in the soil. By uploading the images of plants, the diseases in plants can also be detected. This ease the farmers and helps to get maximum productivity and cultivation.
